GrabZ Features

Locate Items Quickly!

Tap your smart-phone on the NFC tag located on each aisle inside the shop and get a list of all items in that Aisle.

Shopping List!

Create your own shopping lists with GrabZ and quickly locate those items when you tap on the first NFC in the shop. You can have as many lists as you want.
Start Saving time!

Get Promotional Items!

Get the promotional items in the shop on your smart-phone as soon as you tap on any NFC tag in that shop.
See offers exclusively for GrabZ users.

GrabZ-Keeper Features

Manage Aisles!

Manage aisles of your shop with GrabZ-Keeper. You can create and remove Aisles.
You can have as many aisles as you want!

Manage Aisle Items!

With GrabZ-keeper you can scan items and add them to any Aisle. Items that you put on the Aisle will be visible to your customers right away!
It's a blast!

Add Promotional Items!

You can have promotional items exclusive for the GrabZ users. You may add and remove promotional items by scanning them!
